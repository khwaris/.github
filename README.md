# Welcome to Khwaris

Khwaris is a student-led initiative based in Algiers, focused on building software, AI systems, and data-driven platforms. We are a group of passionate students collaborating to learn, experiment, and create impactful technology.

> ⚠️ **Note:** Khwaris is currently an independent student group. We are not affiliated with any national or official organization, and we do not generate income at this stage.

---

## Vision

To grow into a globally impactful technology collective by continuously learning, building, and sharing knowledge in software engineering and artificial intelligence.

---

## Mission

* Build practical, scalable software solutions
* Explore and apply artificial intelligence in real-world scenarios
* Collaborate as a team to learn faster and better
* Focus on outcomes and real impact rather than just deliverables

---

## What We Do

* Develop software projects (web, mobile, and backend systems)
* Experiment with AI/ML models and data-driven applications
* Contribute to open-source and collaborative development
* Share knowledge and improve technical skills as a team

---

## Who We Are

We are students based in Algiers with diverse interests in:

* Software Engineering
* Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
* Data Science
* System Design & Architecture

We are united by curiosity, ambition, and a desire to build meaningful technology.

---

## Current Status

* Early-stage student initiative
* Focused on learning and experimentation
* Building initial projects and internal structure
